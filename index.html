What is LiveAI?

LIVE AI is a fresh multi-campus hybrid hackathon that invites you to join us at either of our host campuses, Duke University (LIVE AI Duke) and Harvard University (LIVE AI Harvard), as well as online (LIVE AI Global) on March 23 - 24. Both LIVE AI Duke and LIVE AI Harvard will provide full in-person hackathon experiences, and all three communities will connect with each other through speaker panels and fun activities.

Rules & Regulations

For the competition, participants may form teams of up to 4 people. Each team must design, develop, and demo products that incorporate at least one of the following three themes: artificial intelligence, blockchain/web3, or financial technology. These products can be developed in various formats, including web, mobile, game, data, XR, among others, and can address a wide range of topics such as art/music, athletics/sports, civic/politics, environment/green initiatives, finance/economics, health/medicine, social/empowerment, etc. For more inspiration and ideas, participants are advised to review the prize package details.

For onsite judging and prizes, you must demo your product at Duke University or Harvard University on March 24.

For online judging and international prizes, submit a demo video lasting 120 - 180 seconds. The video submission deadline is March 24 at 12:00 pm (noon) EDT. You may also submit any supporting documents, files, images, etc.

Prizes

The LIVE AI Hackathon is set to offer $15,000 in prizes across a variety of categories, with each prize being awarded at three major events: LIVE AI Duke, LIVE AI Harvard, and LIVE AI Global. Participants can compete in numerous fields including Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain/Web3, and Financial Technology.

Furthermore, participants utilizing Internet Computer Protocol (ICP) blockchain technology are eligible for the ICP.HUB North America Prize Package, and those employing CESS (Cumulus Encrypted Storage System) technology can compete for the CESS Prize Package, adding more opportunities for innovators to showcase their talents and potentially win significant rewards.
